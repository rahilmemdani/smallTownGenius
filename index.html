Influence Of Schools In Child’s Development

A school is a place where a child spends most of the time right from early childhood years. The period that children spend in school is considered as the most crucial years for a child’s development. Thus, parents should be very careful while choosing a school for their children. The school should not just be confined to academics but it should also be responsible for moral, spiritual, mental, physical as well as cultural development of the children.

While we encourage families to opt for good National and International schools for their children to seek Quality Education, currently we do not have a school shifting funding program.
