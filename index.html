Making India’s tier 2 & tier 3 engineering students industry ready

Out of the 1 million graduates every year, only 20% of students are industry-ready. We are on a mission to make engineering graduates from tier 2 & tier 3 cities startup ready by providing training and mentorship from startup veterans with a personal touch.

Why Tier 2 & Tier 3 Students?

Limited access to the right people.

Limited availability of resources.

Affordability of correct resources.
