Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is this message sent to us?

Around this time of the year, a lot of parents look at shifting their kids to National and International Schools and reach out to the Aga Khan Education Board volunteers to look for more information on where the other students from their centers go. In order to encourage those who can shift and make informed decisions, we are making this information on National and International curriculum schools available.

Are these schools certified? How do I know if they are really good?

Most National and International schools are certified. For more information, please check the school’s website. We also recommend you to do your own survey and network with parents from those schools. Additionally, you can visit ICSE, CBSE, IGCSE and IB website to check if these school’s name is reflecting on their certified list. The link is given above.

Are these schools endorsed by AKEB?

These are list of schools in which AKEB students are enrolled. However this list is not an endorsement of schools, nor is this an exhaustive list of National and International board schools. There can be more schools in your area. Please use this link to explore and find more schools of respective boards if you would like to.

Will I get financial support to shift my child?

While we encourage families to opt for good National and International schools for their children to seek Quality Education, currently we do not have a school shifting funding program.
